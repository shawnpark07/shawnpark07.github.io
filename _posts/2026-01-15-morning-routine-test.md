After years of hitting snooze, I finally discovered a morning routine that sticks.

## The Problem with Most Morning Routines

We've all seen those "5 AM CEO routines" that include meditation, journaling, cold showers, and a 10K run—all before breakfast. For most of us, that's simply not sustainable.

## My Simple Approach

Instead of overhauling everything at once, I focused on just three things:

1. **No phone for the first 30 minutes** - This alone changed everything
2. **One glass of water immediately** - Simple but effective
3. **5 minutes of stretching** - Not yoga, just basic stretches

## Results After 3 Months

- More consistent energy throughout the day
- Better focus in morning meetings
- Actually enjoying mornings for the first time

The key isn't doing more—it's doing less, consistently.
