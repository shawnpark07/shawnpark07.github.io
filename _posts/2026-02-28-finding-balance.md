I used to chase "work-life balance" like it was a destination. Here's why I stopped.

## Balance is Not a State—It's a Practice

The idea that you can achieve perfect balance and maintain it forever is a myth. Life is dynamic, and so should our approach be.

## What I Do Instead

### Seasons, Not Days

Some weeks are work-heavy (product launches, deadlines). Some weeks are life-heavy (family events, personal projects). I plan in seasons, not days.

### Energy Management > Time Management

I stopped asking "Do I have time?" and started asking "Do I have energy?" This shift changed everything.

### Non-Negotiables

I have exactly three:
- Sunday dinners with family
- 30 minutes of exercise daily
- 7 hours of sleep minimum

Everything else is negotiable based on the season.

## The Result

Less guilt, more presence. Whether I'm working or resting, I'm fully there.
